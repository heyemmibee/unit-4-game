Crystal Guessing Game

Guess what!
A random number has been generated and assigned to each of the crystals below.
By clicking on the image of a crystal you will add that specific amount to your to the total score.
Win the game by adding the values of the crystals to make your total score match the random number.
If your total score goes above the random number you lose. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
